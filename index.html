SELL WORKFLOW

How to sell a ticket?

  • NAVIGATE TO SELL PAGE

    Click on Sell button at the top right corner of the webpage.

  • FILL THE FORM

    Enter the details of your ticket, including movie and seller details (autopopulated with logged in user's details but can be modified).

  • VERIFY & SUBMIT

    Make sure you've entered the correct details because the tickets once posted are not editable. After verifying, click on Confirm button.

  • That's it!
    TICKET POSTED

BUY WORKFLOW

How to buy a ticket?

  • NAVIGATE TO BUY PAGE

    Click on Buy button at the top right corner of the webpage.

  • SELECT MOVIE

    To make it easier for the user, there are two types of views: Grid & List. Fiddle around with the filters and find your favorite movie. Click on BUY TICKET button.

  • VENUE, DATE & TIME

    Choose your preferred date. From the list of available venues, choose your preferred show time.

  • SELECT TICKET

    From the list of available tickets, pick your favorite ticket. Click anywhere on the ticket card.

  • REQUEST TICKET

    Ticket details are displayed along with an input field to ask any queries to the seller. After verifying, click on Request Ticket button

  • That's it!
    Requested Ticket

ELEGANT UI

Minimal Flat 2.0 design

Our goal was to develop a simple and elegant user interface. To be simple, the types and number of UI elements must be minimal; all UI elements and their combinations must be understandable for everyday users; and there must be consistency among all UI elements and their combinations. So we adopted Flat 2.0, which combines the best of flat design with additional user interface cues to create website design that’s beautiful and functional.

User Dashboard

Central Place to manage tickets

Wouldn't it be great if there is a central place where a buyer can see the list of tickets he has subscribed to, or a seller to list all the tickets he has posted? We introduce to you, User Dashboard, where you can easily manage your tickets without any hassle.

Simple Search

Search that just works

Our goal was to develop a search box that is clearly visible, quickly recognisable and easy to use. Effective search should subtly guide users in creating and reformulating queries. So we used as-you-type suggestions (auto-suggest) to help users save time, iterate on their searches, and get the results they want.
